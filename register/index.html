Transforming caterpillar into butterfly

First years! Look at this world with your twinkling first year eyes! This world is awesome! There are cars, telephones, music and Youtube and Facebook and aeroplanes. You can fly if you wish to, or go underwater in a submarine. Everything has been made better due to technology (ignore the environmental issues for now).

We want to show you, teach you and learn with you these incredible ideas and technologies. So come join us on this wild ride. We will flap our little butterfly wings wildly in the Robotics Club room on the first floor of Humanities block and fly through jungles and cityscapes (but not Kathmandu its very polluted) with our robots. We will talk about drones and time machines and space exploration. But more importantly we will teach you the basics of making robots. Come with us!

How to enter?
