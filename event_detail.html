Introduction

Robo-Rookies is an event targeted for selecting new members to Robotics Club, IOE paschimanchal campus. This year, unlike before, we are accepting general members from 2nd, 3rd and 4th year so the club is more accessible to everyone.

Workshop for 3 days will be conducted for BEI, BEL, BCT, BAME, and BME. The aim of the workshop is to provide a basic overview of different robotic topics like, CAD designing, PCB designing, Programming, IC circuits etc. Further, the event focuses on the workflow system in the club and past projects.

Tasks will be given at the end of the workshop to every individual. On the basis of task completion and interview, new members will be selected.

What are the benefits of being a club member?

  1. 1, Access to Robotics Lab
  2. 2, Access to Robotic hardware resources(3D printer, Mechanical tools, etc)
  3. 3, Involvement in R&D projects
  4. 4, Personal Development: Skill development, Public Interactions, Networking
  5. 5, Represent the club in different national events.
  6. 6, Teaching and Learning Opportunities
  7. 7, Certificates for Projects completion
