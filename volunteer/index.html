# MISSION:

Robotics Club is established with the strong motive to educate, promote, explore , research and compete in the field of Robotics. It has been providing platform to students who show keen interest in Robotics and Robotics related projects. When its about National and Local level Robotic competitions, Robotics Club is only representative of Paschimanchal Campus.

Robotics Club operates on the core beliefs of a strong work ethic, teamwork, responsibility, integrity, and challenging ourselves. Team members are expected to play fair and help other teams when possible . Students further their interest in engineering and learn how to communicate effectively, work on a team, be a leader, and become active contributors to the club, all which help build professional skills.

# Past Year Work :

Past Year our team has stand First Runner Up in Robocon. Robotics Club Paschimanchal Campus conducted several programs this even semester. Robotics Club organized intensive internal training session Rainy Session and Robot Football Competition. The club has also co-organized World Robotics Olympiad Pokhara Regional Selection with STEM Foundation Nepal.

We are looking for volunteer towards accomplishing our goals of learning, giving back to the club, and building cool robots.

# Selection Criteria:

  1. Must be student of IOE PASS
  2. Must attain Robotics training or should have knowledge on certain field of robotics
  3. Must pass online exam
  4. Pay fee Rs 200

# TASK:

Voluntering in robotics club is guided by following principle :

# 1) Master what you have learn and learn new technology :

-If you want to learn something, read about it. If you want to understand something, write about it. If you want to master something, teach it.

-You learn as much as you can from the people that you work with. That's why you want to surround yourself with the heaviest people that you can possibly get .

We are planing to conduct "Compedition based Robot Programming with C " and "3D Modelling + Printing competition" next odd semester and rainy season in next even semester as usual . You will get chance to teach and share your knowledge among participant under supervision of member.

# 2) Experience is a master teacher, even when it’s not our own :

You will be given chance to share your learning during 15 days meetup programs in any field of robotics .

# 3) Surround yourself with those on the same mission as you :

-If you want to accomplish something fast work alone , but if you want to head long distance and right direction work on team .

-A Great Dream Requires A Great Team.

Get yourself surrounded by likeminded , innovative people .Let create innovative environment in our college and work for common goal . During volunteering you must be bided to the club mission and goal .

# 4)Outside of your comfort zone is where the magic happens.

Get yourself outside of college and participate in different robotics activities representing robotics club . You should represent in outer competition (event which have multiple participation)

# 5) Get yourself involved with management :

-What you need, is an Event, to remember for a lifetime .

Build technical leadership within you . You should manage competition and represent club as per decision by club .

  4. Chance to represent club in different Event.
  5. Teaching
  6. Certificate for Volunteer if eligible
  7. Volunteer Card
  8. Will be upgraded as member
